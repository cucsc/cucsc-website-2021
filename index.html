What is CUCSC?

The Canadian Undergraduate Computer Science Conference is an annual, national conference centered around Computer Science research by Canadian undergraduate students and their research teams.

Our vision is to promote a trans-disciplinary collaborative environment to broaden the horizons of undergraduate Computer Scientists and all our guests.
